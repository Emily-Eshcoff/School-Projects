Digital Learning

We live in the digital age, and helping people navigate online resources is just one step along the path to comprehensive literacy. Whether you need help navigating the web to manage personal tasks from the day-to-day like bills or social media. Or if you are new to the world of computers. We are always here for you. Follow our YouTube Channel to find a wealth of resources!

All of our mentors are also available for Zoom Meetings to make things even more accessible, regardless of where you are or what your needs are. We host regular classes open to the public that you can join at anytime, sign up for our email list or follow any of our social media accounts to stay up to date or get the upcoming schedule and join at your leisure. Or Join our Discord, where an entire community of people wait for your arrival!

Physical Print

We have a whole library of resouces, everything from newspapers, magazines, novels, and short stories. We also host regular meetings at our local library two times a month for our book club. Any of our hard working, passionate mentors are also delighted to make appointments to meet one-on-one to provide a hands on learning experience guided by patience and compassion for anyone from all walks of life, young and old. Everyone is always welcome.
