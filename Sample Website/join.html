We Are All in This Together

We make it our goal every day to make sure everyone feels empowered to be the best version of themselves that they can be. And we work diligently to fulfill our promises. From day one, this has been a volunteer lead organization, and we have no intentions of changing that any time soon. Every resource we have at our disposal goes toward our ultimate goal of bringing every person the gift of literacy and opening their horizons to the wide wonderful world there is to experience.

If you also feel the inspiration we feel, and the potential in a world where people arent left behind, we are always accepting donations of any form. Be it a moment of your time, a box of old books in the attic, or a couple dollars from your coffee table, every bit helps.

Without poeple like you, we couldnt do the important work we do every day.


And of course this is also where, if you've come here to ask for help, you can find a way to be connected to one of our staff who will help place you with the mentor that can change your world. The smallest steps, are what lead to our greatest journeys.
