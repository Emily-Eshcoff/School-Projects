We Are All in This Together

About

We were born from a dream, the founders of this organization all came together from different paths in life to tackle the same issues they all saw at the same time. From these different perspectives Amanda, Zaheed, and Janice have been able to change the lives of hundreds of people, and countless more once you start counting the ripple effect this work has on the communities our alumni are able to newly contribute to. From humble beginings during a would-be one-off summer workshop, dreams became reality. And they continue to this day, at a greater pace than ever.
