Our Mission

There is a pandemic happening the world over. From the poorest villages, to the bourroughs of New York City. People are being left behind. Forgotten. But when we stop and look around us, and take the time to be with our community. We can make a difference to permenantly erase this plague from the face of the Earth.

Illiteracy is not just the inability to read, its also the under-ability to comprehend the reading we are able to do. The consequences of the reality we face is dire.

"" I am, somehow, less interested in the weight and convolutions of Einstein’s brain than in the near certainty that people of equal talent have lived and died in cotton fields and sweatshops. ""

– Stephen Jay Gould, The Panda’s Thumb: More Reflections in Natural History.

The greatest minds of entire generations are being lost to the under-ability to provide the resources that have no scarcity of supply to the people who can use them the most. Our goal is to bridge that gap. We seek a different world. One where all of this is seen as some barbaric and cruel by-gone era.
